Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 21, 2026

At ARC Equity, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy outlines how we handle the information you provide through our Facebook Lead Forms.

1. Information We Collect

When you fill out our form, we collect your name, email address, phone number, and the property address you are interested in selling.

2. How We Use Your Information

This information is used strictly to evaluate your property and provide you with a fair, no-obligation cash offer. We may contact you via phone, text, or email to discuss your offer.

3. Data Security & Third Parties

ARC Equity will never sell, rent, or lease your personal information to third parties. Your data is kept secure and is only accessed by our team to fulfill your request for a cash offer.

4. Your Consent

By submitting your information, you consent to being contacted by ARC Equity. You may opt-out of future communications at any time by simply letting us know during our initial contact.
